Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and clear answers.

1) How much is membership?

We offer two plans:

  • Basic: ₹1499 / month — weekly workouts, community support, monthly check-in.
  • Premium: ₹2999 / month — 1:1 coaching calls, custom nutrition plan, priority support.

2) What are the gym timings?

The gym is open from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily. If you want to book a trainer at a specific time, please schedule an appointment in advance.

3) Are personal trainers available?

Yes — personal trainers are available. Premium membership includes 1:1 coaching. For other plans, you can book trainer sessions separately. To book, fill out the form on the Join page or contact us directly.
